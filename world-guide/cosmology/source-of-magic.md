# Source of Magic - The Atlantean System

## Overview

Magic in HPMOR is not a natural phenomenon but an **engineered system** created by the ancient civilization of Atlantis [Ch. 25, 108]. This explains why magic follows specific rules, respects certain restrictions (like the Interdict of Merlin), and requires DNA-based authentication. Unlike traditional fantasy where magic simply "exists," HPMOR treats magic as an artifact—a technology left behind by a lost civilization.

## Atlantis and the Origin of Magic [EXPLICIT]

### What We Know

1. **Atlantis Created Magic** [Ch. 25, 108]
   - Professor Quirrell: "Atlantis was the name given by us historians to an extraordinarily advanced civilization which existed somewhere in the far distant past of Earth" [Ch. 108]
   - Created the "Source of Magic"—the system that powers all wizarding abilities
   - Had technology/magic far beyond modern wizarding or Muggle capabilities
   - Harry: "So you're saying... that Atlantis, or whoever they were, created the Source of Magic, and they built the Interdict into it" [Ch. 108]

2. **DNA Markers as Authentication** [IMPLIED] [Ch. 25, 108]
   - Magical ability is inherited through DNA
   - Suggests magic checks genetic markers before allowing access
   - Like a security system checking user credentials
   - "Muggle-borns" have magical ancestry somewhere (dormant genes reactivate)
   - Blood purity ideology is misunderstanding of authentication system

3. **Magic as Engineered System** [Ch. 108]
   - Explains why spells require specific wand movements and incantations
   - Why magic respects arbitrary-seeming restrictions (no information from future)
   - Why certain things are impossible (information-theoretic restrictions)
   - Interdict of Merlin built into the system at fundamental level
   - Magic is like computer software with built-in security features

### Why Magic Feels Effortless to Wizards [IMPLIED]

4. **User Interface Design**
   - Once authenticated (magical bloodline), system does heavy lifting
   - Wizard provides intent + correct "password" (incantation + wand movement)
   - Source of Magic executes the actual reality-warping
   - This explains why:
     - Small children can do accidental magic (authenticated users)
     - First-years can Transfigure objects (system handles physics)
     - Adult wizards don't need to understand how Patronus actually works
   - Bad analogy: Wizards are using apps without understanding operating system

5. **Conservation Laws Still Apply** [Ch. 22, 28-29]
   - Magic follows modified physics, not arbitrary rules
   - Transfiguration conserves mass, respects form limitations
   - Time-Turners can't create information from future
   - Suggests Source of Magic enforces consistent physical laws
   - But those laws differ from Muggle physics (Aristotelian broomsticks)

### Evidence of Their Capabilities [Ch. 108]

- Created system that modifies fundamental physics
- Built in restriction system (Interdict of Merlin) that survived millennia
- Made magic heritable through DNA
- Possibly created magical creatures (see Mysteries Unsolved)
- Technology/magic far exceeding modern wizards' understanding

## The Source of Magic Itself [INFERRED]

### What It Does

1. **Reality Warping Engine**
   - Receives requests from authenticated users (wizards)
   - Validates request format (correct spell, wand movement)
   - Executes reality modification
   - Maintains consistency across magical effects

2. **Access Control System**
   - Checks DNA markers for authentication
   - Enforces Interdict of Merlin (powerful magic requires personal instruction)
   - Prevents certain categories of spell (information from future)
   - May limit what individual wizards can access

3. **Conservation Enforcement**
   - Maintains modified physical laws
   - Prevents paradoxes (Time-Turner rules)
   - Enforces Transfiguration limitations
   - Manages magical energy/costs

- If it can be modified or destroyed
- Whether it's on Earth or elsewhere
- If anyone could access its control systems

## Magic as "Hack" or "Exploit" [IMPLIED]

### Harry's Perspective [Ch. 22, 28-29]

- Partial Transfiguration works because Harry understands physics at atomic level
- Other wizards think in terms of "whole objects"—this is limitation of mental model, not magic itself
- Source of Magic respects Harry's understanding (Transfigures atoms, not objects)
- Suggests magic is exploitable by those who understand underlying reality better

### Implications

- Magic may have unintended capabilities (like any complex system)
- Understanding true physics lets you do "impossible" magic
- Most wizards never test boundaries because they accept traditional limitations
- True Patronus works because Harry understood what Dementors really are

## Implications for Fanfiction Writers

### What's Established

- **Magic is Atlantean artifact**: Not natural, created by ancient civilization [EXPLICIT]
- **DNA authentication required**: Explains inheritance patterns [IMPLIED]
- **Interdict of Merlin built-in**: Part of system design, not separate enchantment [EXPLICIT]
- **System has consistent rules**: Can be understood scientifically [EXPLICIT]
- **Exploitable through understanding**: Partial Transfiguration, True Patronus prove this [EXPLICIT]

### What's Open to Interpretation [AMBIGUOUS]

- **What exactly was Atlantis?**: Alien civilization? Time travelers? Advanced humans?
- **Why did they create magic?**: Defense? Exploration? Accident?
- **What happened to them?**: Destroyed? Departed? Evolved?
- **Where is Source of Magic located?**: Another dimension? Orbit? Underground?
- **Can it be modified or controlled?**: Could modern wizards hack the system?
- **Did they create magical creatures too?**: Dragons, phoenixes, Dementors?
- **Are there other magical systems?**: Did other civilizations create different magic?

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Treating magic as natural**: In HPMOR, magic is technology, not natural phenomenon
2. **Ignoring DNA inheritance**: Magic checks bloodline, it's not random gift
3. **Missing the implications**: If magic is engineered, it has limits, exploits, possibly backdoors
4. **Forgetting Interdict**: Built into Source of Magic, can't be circumvented easily
5. **Atlantis deus ex machina**: Don't resolve plots with "Atlantis left instructions"—that's lazy

### Story Hooks Writers Can Explore

- **Discovering Atlantean ruins**: What other artifacts exist?
- **Finding Source of Magic's location**: Could it be studied? Modified?
- **Alternative magical systems**: Did other ancient civilizations create different magic?
- **Atlantean descendants**: Are there bloodlines with special access/knowledge?
- **Magical creatures' origin**: If Atlantis made magic system, did they create phoenixes? Dementors?
- **Exploiting the system**: What other "hacks" are possible with proper understanding?
