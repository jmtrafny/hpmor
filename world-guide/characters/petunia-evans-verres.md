# Petunia Evans-Verres

## Basic Information

- **Full Name**: Petunia Evans-Verres (née Evans)
- **House**: N/A (Muggle)
- **Role**: Harry's adoptive mother; Lily's sister; beautiful through magic; tragic figure
- **First Appearance**: Ch. 1
- **Chapter Count**: Appears in early chapters (1-6), mentioned later, appears briefly Ch. 36, 91

## HPMOR vs Canon Divergences

**CRITICAL DIFFERENCES:**

1. **Beautiful**: Lily magically transformed her with dangerous potion. Canon Petunia was plain/harsh-looking.

2. **Married to Scientist**: Michael Verres-Evans, not Vernon Dursley. Different life path.

3. **Loving to Harry**: Genuinely cares for him, not abusive. Complicated but affectionate.

4. **Scarred by Sister's Death**: Lily's death traumatized her deeply. Fear for Harry.

5. **Wanted Magic**: Begged Lily for transformation, was desperate to be magical like her sister.

6. **Complex Relationship with Magic**: Protective but distant. Fears what happened to Lily.

7. **Took Husband's Name**: Michael took "Verres-Evans" - she was more valuable, higher status.

## Personality & Motivations

### Core Traits [EXPLICIT]

- **Beautiful**: Magically transformed by Lily. "People were nice to me." [Ch. 1]

- **Protective**: Deeply worried about Harry going to magical world. Fears losing him like Lily. [Ch. 1, 7]

- **Scarred**: Sister's death traumatized her. "I learned what her magic brought her in the end." [Ch. 1]

- **Wanted magic**: Begged Lily for years. "I begged her. For years, I begged her." [Ch. 1]

- **Loving mother**: Genuinely cares for Harry despite distance. [Ch. 7, 36]

- **Elegant**: Carries herself with grace, beauty. Different from bitter canon Petunia. [Throughout]

### Voice & Speech Patterns

**Vocabulary**: Elegant, educated (Oxford professor's wife), emotional

**Common Phrases**:
- "My love" (to Michael or Harry)
- "I know I can't win arguments with you, but please..." (to Michael)
- Worried maternal language
- Elegant but vulnerable speech

**Emotional Range**:
- With Harry: Loving, protective, worried
- About magic: Fearful, scarred, protective
- About Lily: Sad, regretful, traumatized
- With Michael: Loving, sometimes frustrated

### Dialogue Samples

**Begging Lily:**
> "I begged her. For years, I begged her. Lily had always been prettier than me, and I'd...been mean to her, because of that, and then she got magic, can you imagine how I felt? And I begged her to use some of that magic on me so that I could be pretty too, even if I couldn't have her magic, at least I could be pretty." [Ch. 1]

**To Harry:**
> "Oh, Harry, I do love you, always remember that." [Ch. 7]

**About Magic:**
> "I learned what her magic brought her in the end—" [Ch. 1]

## Writer Notes

### Common Mistakes

1. **Canon bitterness**: This Petunia is transformed, different life
2. **Pure villain**: She's traumatized, protective, complex
3. **Missing beauty**: Described as beautiful, elegant throughout
4. **Forgetting her tragedy**: Lily's death scarred her deeply

### Key Nuances

- Wanted magic desperately, settled for magical beauty
- Loves Harry but scared of losing him
- Transformation came at great cost (dangerous potion)
- Beauty changed how people treated her - significant
- Complex feelings about magic: wonder, fear, loss

### Writing Successfully

- Elegant voice befitting transformed beauty
- Show vulnerability under elegance
- Protective maternal instinct
- Fear for Harry underlying interactions
- Remember she took great risk for beauty (dangerous potion)
