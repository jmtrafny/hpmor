## Character Arcs to Continue

### Harry Potter [EXPLICIT & IMPLIED]

1. **Long-Term Quest to Defeat Death** [Ch. 96, 122]
   - **Setup**: Harry accepted Potter family quest: "Death is my enemy, and I will defeat it" [Ch. 96]
   - **Status at story end**: Resurrected Hermione (proof of concept), but death still exists
   - **Unexplored**: How does Harry pursue this? Research? Political change? Finding other magical solutions?
   - **Story hooks**:
     - Researching resurrection magic systematically
     - Creating institution dedicated to life extension
     - Seeking other Deathly Hallows or immortality methods
     - Collaborating with Nicolas Flamel (if still alive)
     - Bringing Muggle medical science to magical world

2. **Becoming Scientific Revolutionary** [Throughout]
   - **Setup**: Harry wants to understand and revolutionize magical world
   - **Status at story end**: Major discoveries (partial Transfiguration, True Patronus) but just beginning
   - **Unexplored**: What else can be discovered? How does he spread scientific thinking?
   - **Story hooks**:
     - Teaching scientific method at Hogwarts (Year Two and beyond)
     - Publishing magical research journals
     - Creating experiments to map edge cases of magic
     - Discovering more "impossible" magic through understanding
     - Building bridges between magical and Muggle science

3. **Political Power and Responsibility** [Ch. 82-84, 122]
   - **Setup**: Head of Noble House Potter, established House Granger, saved Hermione
   - **Status at story end**: 11 years old with massive political influence
   - **Unexplored**: How does he use/manage this power? What are consequences?
   - **Story hooks**:
     - Learning to navigate Wizengamot politics
     - Using House Potter influence for reform
     - Dealing with enemies made during story
     - Balancing power with ethics (consequentialism in practice)
     - Mentoring Hermione as head of House Granger

4. **Relationship with Hermione** [Throughout, especially Ch. 89-90, 122]
   - **Setup**: Deep friendship with romantic undertones; Harry resurrected her
   - **Status at story end**: Hermione swore oath of friendship to Harry [Ch. 122]
   - **Unexplored**: How does relationship develop? Romance? Platonic partnership?
   - **Story hooks**:
     - Navigating friendship post-resurrection
     - Hermione's perspective on having died
     - Romantic development (or deliberate friendship)
     - Partnership in defeating death
     - Working together on scientific magical research

## Mysteries Writers Can Explore

### Atlantis and Magic's Origin [AMBIGUOUS]

18. **What Was Atlantis?** [Ch. 25, 108]
    - **Setup**: Ancient civilization that created Source of Magic
    - **Status in canon**: Deliberately unexplained [See mysteries-unsolved.md]
    - **Open for writers**: Literally anything consistent with "created magic, used DNA markers, fell/disappeared"
    - **Story hooks**:
      - Discovering Atlantean ruins
      - Finding other Atlantean artifacts
      - Learning Atlantean language or technology
      - Meeting Atlantean descendants (if any exist)
      - Understanding why they created magic

19. **Location of Source of Magic** [AMBIGUOUS]
    - **Setup**: Magic is engineered system somewhere
    - **Status in canon**: Location never mentioned
    - **Open for writers**: Could be anywhere
    - **Story hooks**:
      - Harry's quest to find Source of Magic
      - Studying magic's mechanics directly
      - Discovering control systems or interfaces
      - Modifying magic's rules (dangerous!)
      - Understanding magic at deepest level

20. **Other Atlantean Creations** [AMBIGUOUS]
    - **Setup**: If Atlantis created magic, what else did they create?
    - **Status in canon**: Never mentioned
    - **Open for writers**: Any Atlantean artifact/location/system
    - **Story hooks**:
      - Finding Atlantean library or database
      - Discovering other magical systems (different from current magic)
      - Atlantean AI or guardian constructs
      - Maps or records of Atlantis
      - Technology more advanced than magic

### Souls and Afterlife [AMBIGUOUS]

21. **Is There an Afterlife?** [Ch. 39, 40]
    - **Setup**: Dumbledore believes yes, Quirrell found no evidence, Harry agnostic
    - **Status in canon**: Deliberately unanswered
    - **Open for writers**: Can answer either way without contradicting canon
    - **Story hooks**:
      - Harry designing experiment to test for afterlife
      - Discovering evidence one way or other
      - Using magic to communicate with dead (if possible)
      - Philosophical implications of answer
      - How answer affects quest to defeat death

22. **What Are Souls Exactly?** [AMBIGUOUS]
    - **Setup**: Horcruxes reference "souls," Dementors affect them, but nature unclear
    - **Status in canon**: Never explained
    - **Open for writers**: Define souls however makes sense
    - **Story hooks**:
      - Scientific study of souls
      - Discovering soul mechanics
      - Soul magic beyond Horcruxes
      - Connection between consciousness and soul
      - Whether souls are magical constructs or something else

## How to Use This File

### For Story Ideas:
1. Pick a thread that interests you
2. Check it doesn't contradict established HPMOR canon
3. Develop it in direction that makes sense
4. Remember: These are OPEN, not determined

### For Character Development:
- Continue arcs started in HPMOR
- Show growth and change
- Explore consequences of story events
- Give characters new challenges

### For Worldbuilding:
- Answer mysteries HPMOR left open
- Expand on barely-mentioned elements
- Create new areas consistent with established world
- Add depth without contradicting canon

## What NOT to Do

**Don't**:
- Contradict explicit HPMOR canon
- Ignore major story events (Hermione's resurrection, Voldemort's death, etc.)
- Resurrect Voldemort without excellent explanation
- Forget character growth that already happened
- Violate established magic rules without reason

**Do**:
- Build on what's established
- Respect character development
- Answer open questions creatively
- Create new content in unexplored areas
- Have fun expanding the world
