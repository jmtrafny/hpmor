## Index Structure

This index provides a chapter-by-chapter breakdown of Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality, extracting key information to support the World Guide construction. Each chapter entry includes:

- **Chapter Number/Title**: Official chapter designation and name
- **Primary Characters**: Main characters appearing or discussed
- **Magic Concepts**: Spells, magical objects, or magical theory introduced or explored
- **Locations**: Key settings within the chapter
- **Organizations**: Groups, Houses, or institutions referenced
- **Plot Summary**: 1-2 sentence overview of key events
- **Themes**: Rationality concepts, philosophical ideas, or character development
- **Notable Quotes**: 1-2 representative quotes capturing character voice or key themes
