# Year One Timeline (1991-1992)

## Overview

HPMOR takes place entirely during Harry Potter's first year at Hogwarts (ages 11-12), from September 1991 to June 1992. This timeline tracks major events month-by-month based on chapter content and source index.

## September 1991: Arrival and Sorting

### September 1 - Hogwarts Express [Ch. 7-10]
- Harry meets Draco Malfoy, begins reforming him [Ch. 7-8]
- Meets Hermione Granger on train [Ch. 9]
- Observes fellow first-years [Ch. 10]

### First Week - Sorting and Orientation [Ch. 9, 12-13]
- Sorting ceremony: Harry to Ravenclaw, Hermione to Ravenclaw, Draco to Slytherin [Ch. 12]
- Dumbledore announces Forbidden Forest forbidden, third-floor corridor deadly [Ch. 12]
- First classes begin [Ch. 13-15]
- Harry receives Time-Turner from Dumbledore [Ch. 17]

### Mid-September - Early Conflicts [Ch. 16-20]
- First Defence class with Professor Quirrell [Ch. 16]
- Hermione bullied by older students [Ch. 16-19]
- Harry and Hermione begin rivalry/friendship [Ch. 17-19]

### Late September - Army Formation [Ch. 21-22]
- Professor Quirrell announces student armies [Ch. 21]
- Dragon Army (General Draco), Sunshine Regiment (General Hermione), Chaos Legion (General Harry) [Ch. 22]
- First army battles begin [Ch. 22-34]

## October 1991: Experiments and Escalation

### Early October - Scientific Experiments [Ch. 22-23]
- Harry teaches Draco experimental method [Ch. 22-23]
- Blood purity testing experiments [Ch. 23]
- Draco learns blood purism is false [Ch. 23]

### Mid-October - Draco's Crisis [Ch. 24]
- Draco casts torture hex on Harry [Ch. 24]
- Harry's hand damaged, treated by Madam Pomfrey [Ch. 24]
- Harry blackmails Dumbledore to avoid exposing Draco [Ch. 24]
- Draco and Harry reach uneasy friendship [Ch. 24]

### Late October - Army Battles Continue [Ch. 25-34]
- Escalating tactical complexity in army battles
- Multiple battles across October-November
- Hermione develops leadership skills
- Harry experiments with battle magic innovations

## November 1991: Patronus and Politics

### Early November - Hermione vs. Bullies [Ch. 27-29]
- Hermione receives mysterious notes telling her where to fight bullies
- Defeats older students through preparation
- Gains confidence and reputation [Ch. 27-29]

### Mid-November - Patronus Training Begins [Ch. 43-46]
- Harry learns Patronus Charm theory
- Discovers True Patronus (life and humanity, not happy thought)
- Confronts Dementor, kills it with True Patronus [Ch. 45-46]
- Major philosophical breakthrough

### Late November - Political Tensions [Ch. 47-50]
- Quirrell's influence on Harry grows
- Dumbledore watches Harry warily
- Army battles reach climax [Ch. 28-34]

## December 1991: Winter Break and Mysteries

### Early December - Triple Tie [Ch. 33-34]
- Final army battle results in 254-254-254 three-way tie
- Blaise Zabini orchestrated the perfect balance
- Army system suspended temporarily

### Mid-December - Azkaban Planning [Ch. 49-54]
- Quirrell reveals plan to rescue innocent prisoner from Azkaban
- Harry learns about Bellatrix Black [Ch. 51]
- Secret planning and Time-Turner complications [Ch. 51-54]

### Late December - Azkaban Rescue [Ch. 55-57]
- Harry and Quirrell infiltrate Azkaban
- Harry's True Patronus blinds Dementors
- Bellatrix Black rescued - first successful Azkaban escape
- Major political crisis ensues [Ch. 55-58]

### December 25 - Christmas [Ch. 63]
- Harry receives invisibility cloak anonymously
- Note: "This belonged to your father. Use it well"
- Mystery of sender (revealed later as Dumbledore via Santa Claus)

## January 1992: Investigations and Tensions

### Early January - Return from Break [Ch. 58-63]
- Students return to Hogwarts
- Azkaban breakout investigated
- Aurors increase security
- Quirrell under suspicion

### Mid-January - Escalating Mysteries [Ch. 64-72]
- Hat and Cloak missions (Harry's vigilante activities)
- Mysterious happenings around Hogwarts
- Quirrell's tracking Charms on Draco revealed [Ch. 79]

### Late January - Hermione's Research [Ch. 73-75]
- Hermione obsesses over Draco (False Memory Charm suspected later)
- Studies excessively about magical threats
- Begins showing signs of paranoia

## February 1992: Tragedy Strikes

### February - Supposed Duel [Ch. 77-79]
- Draco allegedly duels Hermione at midnight
- Hermione allegedly attacks Draco from behind with Blood-Cooling Charm
- Draco found near death, saved by Quirrell
- Hermione arrested by Aurors [Ch. 79]

### Hermione's Trial Preparation [Ch. 79-80]
- Veritaserum interrogation confirms false memories
- Legilimency shows months of obsession
- Harry, Dumbledore, Snape, McGonagall investigate
- Suspect False Memory Charms and conspiracy
- Trial scheduled for noon next day [Ch. 79]

## March 1992: Trial and Aftermath

### Hermione's Trial [Ch. 80-84]
- Wizengamot convenes at Lucius Malfoy's request
- Blood debt recognized for attempted murder of Malfoy heir
- Lucius demands 100,000 Galleons compensation
- Harry offers entire Potter fortune
- Hermione becomes debt-ward to House Potter [Ch. 82-83]
- Harry pays blood debt, Hermione released to his custody [Ch. 83]

### Post-Trial [Ch. 84-86]
- Educational Decrees passed by Board of Governors [Ch. 98]
- Nine Aurors deployed to Hogwarts as Auxiliary Protective Force
- House Points system suspended
- Hermione struggles with trauma and debt-ward status

## April 1992: Conspiracies Revealed

### Early April - Escalating Danger [Ch. 87-97]
- Multiple suspicious incidents
- Quirrell's condition worsening
- Dumbledore investigates Voldemort's possible return
- Harry suspects multiple conspiracies overlapping

### Mid-April - Hermione's Death [Ch. 89]
- Hermione killed by troll in girls' bathroom
- Harry finds body, devastated
- Swears to resurrect her
- Begins researching resurrection magic

### Late April - Memorial and Planning [Ch. 90-94]
- Hermione's memorial service
- Harry preserves body with magic
- Dumbledore reveals prophecy to Harry
- Harry learns he must defeat Voldemort

## May 1992: The Final Exams (Literal and Figurative)

### Early May - Escalating Confrontations [Ch. 95-105]
- Harry investigates Hermione's death
- Quirrell's true nature increasingly suspicious
- Multiple suspects: Quirrell, Snape, Voldemort
- Dumbledore prepares defenses

### Mid-May - Revelations [Ch. 106-108]
- Harry deduces Quirrell is Voldemort
- Confrontation in Hogwarts
- Voldemort kidnaps Harry via Portkey
- Taken to graveyard

### Final Days - The Final Exam [Ch. 109-113]
- Voldemort summons 36 Death Eaters to graveyard
- Harry faces naked, wandless, facing Voldemort + Death Eaters
- 60-hour reader solution challenge [hp-exam.tex]
- Harry's solution: Transfigure Voldemort's gun into nanowire, kill Death Eaters
- Voldemort defeated, spirit destroyed
- Harry Obliviates Death Eaters

## June 1992: Resolution and New Beginnings

### Early June - Aftermath [Ch. 114-119]
- Harry returns to Hogwarts with Voldemort's body
- Hermione resurrected via Philosopher's Stone and Transfiguration [Ch. 119]
- Hermione becomes first unicorn Animagus
- House Granger founded - Hermione first Muggle-born Noble House founder

### Mid-June - Political Changes [Ch. 120-122]
- Snape departs Hogwarts [Ch. 121]
- McGonagall becomes Headmistress
- Quirrell/Voldemort officially dead
- Wizarding Britain believes Voldemort defeated by "mysterious attacker"
- Harry and Hermione conceal truth about events

### Late June - End of Year [Ch. 120-122]
- Final feast
- House Cup awarded (details vary)
- Students depart for summer
- Harry plans future: destroy Dementors, reform wizarding world, pursue immortality research
- Hermione plans her own future as House Granger founder
- Draco transformed by year's events

## Time-Turner Complications [Throughout]

Harry's Time-Turner use creates complex timeline:
- Can go back up to 6 hours
- Used for attending multiple classes simultaneously
- Used for investigations and secret missions
- Creates self-consistent time loops
- Key to Azkaban rescue and other missions
- Protective shell added after public use incidents [Ch. 14, 18]

## Key Recurring Events

**Weekly**:
- Classes: Transfiguration, Charms, Potions, Defence, Herbology, Astronomy
- Army battles (until suspension)
- Study sessions with Draco (Saturdays)
- Hermione's research and homework

**Monthly/Periodic**:
- Army battles (pre-suspension)
- Hat and Cloak missions
- Secret meetings with Quirrell
- Dumbledore monitoring Harry

## HPMOR vs Canon Timeline Differences

**Major Divergences**:
- **Hermione dies and is resurrected** (completely non-canon)
- **Hermione to Ravenclaw**, not Gryffindor
- **No Quidditch plotline** (minimal role)
- **Student armies replace Quidditch as competition**
- **Azkaban breakout in first year** (canon: later)
- **Voldemort defeated in first year** (canon: book 7)
- **True Patronus discovery** (HPMOR innovation)
- **Hermione founds Noble House** (impossible in canon)

**Retained**:
- School year September-June structure
- Classes and professors (though personalities differ)
- Hogwarts setting and Houses
- Some key events (adapted heavily)

## Implications for Fanfiction Writers

**What's Established**:
- Entire HPMOR story is Harry's first year (1991-1992)
- September: Sorting, armies formed
- December: Azkaban rescue
- February: Hermione arrested
- March: Hermione's trial
- April: Hermione dies
- May: Final confrontation with Voldemort
- June: Hermione resurrected, year concludes
- Time-Turner used throughout for investigations

**What's Open to Interpretation** [AMBIGUOUS]:
- Exact dates within months
- Details of classes not shown
- Other students' activities parallel to main plot
- What happens during time-skips
- Summer plans after year one

**Useful for Stories**:
- Month-by-month structure for pacing
- Time-Turner complications for plot
- Major events as timeline anchors
- Parallel investigations and mysteries
- Character development arcs across year
